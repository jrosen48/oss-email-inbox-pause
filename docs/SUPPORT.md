## Common Issues

### Add-in Not Appearing
1. Ensure you've granted all required permissions
2. Refresh your Outlook window
3. Check if the add-in is enabled in Settings â†’ Add-ins
4. Try signing out and back into Outlook

### Emails Not Being Paused
1. Check that the "Pause" button shows as enabled
2. Verify the "Paused" folder exists in your mailbox
3. Check your internet connection
4. Look for error messages in the add-in taskpane

### Authentication Errors
1. Try signing out of the add-in and signing back in
2. Clear your browser cache (for Outlook Web)
3. Revoke and re-grant permissions in your Microsoft account settings
4. Ensure your Microsoft account is active

### Paused Emails Not Returning to Inbox
1. Click the "Resume" button
2. Wait a few moments for processing
3. Refresh your inbox
4. Check the "Paused" folder to verify emails are still there

## FAQ

**Q: Is this add-in free?**
A: Yes! It's completely free and open source.

**Q: Do you read my emails?**
A: No. The add-in only moves emails between folders. It does not read, store, or analyze email content.

**Q: Does this work on mobile?**
A: Currently, the add-in is designed for Outlook Web and Desktop. Mobile support may be added in the future.

**Q: What happens to my data if the service goes offline?**
A: Your emails remain safe in your mailbox. The "Paused" folder and all emails stay in your Outlook account.

**Q: Can I use this for multiple email accounts?**
A: Yes, but you'll need to add the add-in to each account separately.

**Q: How is this different from Boomerang?**
A: This is a free, open-source alternative to Boomerang's inbox pause feature. It has fewer features but costs $0 instead of $180/year.
